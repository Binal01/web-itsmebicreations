About ItsMeBiCreations

ItsMeBiCreations was started in December 2020 by Binal. My Love for hair accessories from childhood is now become a dream for me! I started sewing since childhood but never thought to make it a business! I love making baby headbands for cute little babies, getting creative with patterns and strived to create a comfortable fit that babies could actually keep on her head. Babies looks adorable in each baby headbands.

Our goal is that weather your hair’s big and curly or straight and fine, we’ve got your scrunchies game covered with our wide selection of scrunchies. Making great hair super simple and comfortable for busy women!

ItsMeBiCreations is a brand that focus on helping everyone find their flair by exploring each personality through accessorizing. With our personal style guides and how-tos, hair accessories can alter an appearance from a regular attire to a stylish fashion mogul for all ages!
